Spot UV Cards

Give your cards a luxurious finish with Spot UV. We apply a shiny raised layer to specific areas like text or graphics over a smooth matte base. This subtle but powerful effect adds texture, shine, and style. Perfect for brands that want elegance and impact in one card.

Spot UV helps important details stand out and leaves a lasting impression Ideal for premium branding, creative professionals, and corporate use. Available with custom shapes, sizes, and high-quality paper options.
